The Era of The Big Three

What is The Big Three?

What is The Big Three you ask? Well, for those of us who are familiar with anime, The Big Three is a term used to describe the three most popular running anime series during their golden age in Shonen Jump's mid 2000s period.
These three are also some of the most iconic anime in the industry sporting large numbers of sales in both of their respective anime and manga.
Personally, I have to say I've always preferred Bleach and Naruto, but One Piece has it's charms. Let's talk a bit about each one:
