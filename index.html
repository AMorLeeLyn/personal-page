The Era of The Big Three

What is The Big Three?

What is The Big Three you ask? Well, for those of us who are familiar with anime, The Big Three is a term used to describe the three most popular running anime series during their golden age in Shonen Jump's mid 2000s period.
These three are also some of the most iconic anime in the industry sporting large numbers of sales in both of their respective anime and manga.
Personally, I have to say I've always preferred Bleach and Naruto, but One Piece has it's charms. Let's talk a bit about each one:

BLEACH

"We are taught never to shed tears.
For to shed tears means that the body has been defeated by emotion.
And to us, this simple act of crying proves, without question, that the existence of emotion is nothing but a burden.”

-- Rukia Kuchiki

Synopsis:

High school student Ichigo Kurosaki, who has the ability to see ghosts, gains soul reaper powers from Rukia Kuchiki and sets out to save the world from "Hollows". ... Ichigo's life completely changes one day when he and his two sisters are attacked by an evil, hungry and tormented spirit known as a Hollow.

NARUTO

“When people are protecting something truly precious to them.
They truly can become…as strong as they need to be!”

-- Haku

Timeline:

  • Manga - Ran from September 21, 1999 to November 10, 2014 with 72 volumes in total
  • Anime - Ran from October 3, 2002 - March 23, 2017
    1. Naruto - First aired on October 3, 2002 and ended February 8, 2007 with 220 total episodes
    2. Naruto Shippuden - Aired from February 15, 2007 to March 23, 2017 and had a total of 500 episodes

Synopsis:

Naruto follows the adventures of a teen ninja-in-training, Naruto Uzumaki. Orphaned at birth, Naruto is a practical joker who will do anything for attention. His grades at the Ninja Academy suck, and he is shunned by most of the adults in the village. Naruto’s secret? His body is the living prison for the Nine-Tailed Fox Demon that almost destroyed the Village Hidden in the Leaves 15 years ago. The series is an epic journey, as the teen Naruto grows from a bratty misfit to a powerful ninja with the potential to become the next Hokage.

ONE PIECE

“When Do You Think People Die?
When They Are Shot In The Heart With A Pistol? No.
When They Are Ravaged By An Uncurable Disease? No.
When They Drink A Soup Made From Poisonous Mushrooms? No.
It’s When... They Are Forgotten!”

-- Dr.Hiriluk

Timeline:

  • Manga - Ran from July 22, 1997 to present with 101 volumes and counting!
  • Anime - Ran from October 20, 1999 to present with a whooping 1008 episodes and is still going strong

Synopsis:

In a fantastical sea-oriented world full of pirates and giant sea creatures, hidden treasure, ships, and island towns, a notorious pirate named Gold Roger hid his most valued treasure deep within the infamous and extremely dangerous sea route known as the Grand Line. Before he was executed, his final words were, "My treasures? If you want it, I'll let you have it. Look for it; I left all of it at that place." These words sent many to the seas, chasing their dreams, headed toward the Grand Line, in search of One Piece. Thus, the story follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young boy, endowed with stretching powers from the legendary gum-gum devil's fruit, who wants nothing more than to travel to the Grand Line, find One Piece, and become "King of the Pirates." Inspired by his childhood hero, a scruffy but cordial pirate named Shanks, Luffy will stop at nothing to achieve this dream of his. Together, Luffy and his crew journey through many high-seas adventures, face off against wicked pirates, and encounter all kinds of colorful characters during their ongoing search for One Piece!
